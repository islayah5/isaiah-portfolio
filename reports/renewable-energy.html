Key Strategic Insights

Lead Quality "Independence" Beats "Savings"

Ads focusing on "Grid Independence" and "Security" had a 40% higher click-through rate than generic "Save Money" creative. Homeowners are buying peace of mind, not just lower bills.

Geo-Targeting The "Suburban Belt" Opportunity

Analysis shows a dense cluster of high-intent leads in the outer suburban belt (zones 3 & 4), correlating with 15k+ sq ft lots. We need to double down on direct mail in these specific zip codes.

Sales Friction Simplified Proposal UX

Reducing the digital proposal from 12 pages to a 3-page "Executive Summary" style reduced time-to-sign by 3 days.

Technical Breakdown

Installation Velocity: Average install time dropped from 45 days to 32 days due to improved permit expediting in key counties.
Battery Attach Rate: 65% of new solar customers also purchased battery back-up, a 15% increase QoQ, driven by the "Blackout Protection" campaign.
Referral Coefficient: Customer referral rate sits at 1.8, meaning every 10 customers bring in 18 new leads organically.

Q1 Directives

• Scale "Independence" Creative: Roll out the new ad angle to all secondary markets.
• Optimize Referral Program: Launch the "Solar Ambassador" app to streamline organic referrals.
• Permit Automation: Implement the new AI-driven permit filing system to further reduce install times.
